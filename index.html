About  Me

I am a Registered Nurse with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Perpetual Help System DALTA – Calamba Campus, having passed the Philippine Nurse Licensure Examination in February 2026. With hands-on clinical experience and a strong foundation in patient care, I bring a detail-oriented, compassionate, and highly organized approach to healthcare. I am also HIPAA-certified, ensuring strict compliance with patient confidentiality and data security standards.

As I transition into a Medical Virtual Assistant role, I offer a blend of clinical knowledge and administrative expertise, including patient scheduling, medical records management (EHR/EMR), insurance verification, and medical documentation. I am skilled in communication across phone, email, and chat, and thrive in fast-paced environments where accuracy and efficiency are essential. My goal is to support healthcare providers by delivering reliable, high-quality virtual assistance while maintaining excellent patient-centered service.
